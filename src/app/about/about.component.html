How does the Ask Trinity bot work?

Based on ChatGPT's language processing model, the Ask Trinity bot interrogates data from the Trinity College London website www.trinitycollege.com and a number of syllabus, regulation, and guidance documents to auto-generate answers to your questions. If the answer is vague or unhelpful; we recommend asking a more specific question. Over time the quality of answers will improve as the technology learns from your questions.
